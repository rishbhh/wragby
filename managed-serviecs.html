Managed Services

Wragby Managed Service

As an Azure Expert Managed Service Provider, Wragby provides full-spectrum IT support services for your organization irrespective of your industry vertical. Our services are designed to suit your specification and ensure you remain in full control of your processes while having the reliability that guarantees you Peace of Mind. We help you manage your Azure Subscription securely and efficiently offering a full life cycle management for the various services deployed on the Cloud Platform.

Having moved to Azure, the possibilities become endless. Our Team of Azure acclaimed Architects and Consultants will work with you to ensure the realization of your Digital Transformation imperatives. We do this through our array of Managed Services Plan ranging from the Free-Tier which includes Break/Fix Support, Patch and Update Management, Business Services Monitoring, and 30-day Backups to our paid plans with more advanced scenarios such as Cost Optimization, Deep Security and Application Modernization

Our Managed Service Offer

  • 24 x 7 support
  • Mobile Device Management
  • Datacenter Management
  • Application Delivery Services
  • Email Continuity
  • On-site Maintenance visits
  • Full IT support (Patching, updates, configuration management, identity management)
  • Back up and Disaster Recovery
  • Deep Security (WAF, DDos, Threat analytics, anti-virus, anti-malware, email, and web protection)
  • Infrastructure Monitoring and Alerting, plus more.
