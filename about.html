The Great Wragby Story

We, started out as an idea birthed in 2014 by a group of industry leaders, with the aim to bridge the gap between businesses and technology. That idea gave rise to the creation of our unique Brand, Wragby Business Solutions and Technologies Limited.

Today, Wragby is a proven industry leader with expertise in Cloud Infrastructure, Artificial Intelligence, Data, Software Engineering, Product Development, Modern Workplace, Business Applications, Digital Advisory and Managed Services in West Africa. Our company is a tier-one technology solutions provider with unique competencies, advanced specializations, Awards, and various industry-tailored Products. These affirm our dedication to transforming businesses via innovation and making the world a better place through technology.

Vision

To make the world a better place to live through technology

Mission

Be the preferred ICT partner to our customers by simplifying the complexity of ICT services and solutions through innovative, agile, and cost-effective strategies that consistently deliver quality which exceeds our customer expectations.

Ethics & Social Responsibility

We aim to operate safely, protect our staff, and deal ethically with our stakeholders, partners, and the public. We are committed to making a positive impact to the society in which we operate by running a responsible business.

Values

Our values remain the foundation of all we do and every decision we make as a company.

Teamwork : We seek to understand how we can best support each other and make choices that put team before individual performance.

Honesty : We do what we say and are transparent with our employees, customers, partners, and stakeholders.

Respect : We treat each other as individuals whose value in our organization goes beyond position, title, and level.

Innovation : We constantly strive to redefine the standard of excellence in everything we do. Therefore, we are open to ideas that challenge the conventional views while striving to improve the society's ever-changing needs.

Commitment and Accountability : While we constantly look to improve ourselves and our service, cultivating our knowledge, skill, and attitudes to achieve excellence, we hold ourselves accountable for our actions in support of our mission by being the best we can be through consistency in our service delivery and going the extra mile to get the job done.

Empowerment : Through constant learning, we aim to empower each other and support one another's goals and self-actualization, while collaborating toward common goals.

OurApproach

We empower our customers to fully optimize their business operations by providing best-in-class business and technology solutions which:

  • Embrace innovation
  • Reduce risk
  • Control costs
  • Meets the highest level of Information Security and Compliance standards

At Wragby, we design, build, and operate our customers’ IT estates across all major technology platforms, irrespective of technology stack or deployment model. We partner with our customers at every stage of their IT investment lifecycle from initial strategy development and technology assessment to design, procurement, and implementation. Enabling them to modernize applications, build new products and adopt innovative technologies.

Wragby is obsessed with delivering outstanding customer service; in fact, we anchored our entire company vision on delivering services that makes our customers smile. Our talented team of people and our structured, intelligent business processes enable us to achieve this.

Technology Partners

By combining our strengths and experience, we partner strategically with top technology providers to deliver solutions that yields the best outcomes for our customers.
