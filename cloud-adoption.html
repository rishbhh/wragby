Cloud Adoption And Change Management

Engage Your workforce in new ways of working

New technology offers the potential to achieve operational excellence but only people can make it happen.

Growth in enterprise IT spending for cloud-based offerings has become faster than non-cloud spending in recent times. The drive is not just because of cost savings from a Capex perspective, but rather value from speed in delivery of new or changed products and services, in line with the ever-changing customer needs and market dynamics.

Change can be quite tricky when planning and implementing cloud technologies. As an organization, to therefore enjoy full ROI on your cloud services investment, you must invest in managing the impact of technology change on users

Cloud Adoption and change management supports your organization’s transition to new technologies by focusing on how your users and departments operate.

The Wragby Acceler8 Program

At Wragby we have perfected our own flavor of adoption and change management methodology, Wragby Acceler8. Based on the Prosci® ADKAR (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement) methodology, we also incorporated several Microsoft partners tools.

Typically, the Wragby process includes these key steps.

  • Identify the key decision, Makers.
  • Work with client to appoint change ambassadors as internal advocates.
  • Ask executive sponsor to help communicate the changes the client wants to see and why.
  • Conduct a Business Decision Maker Assessment Workshop to sort out the differences between stated needs of executives and that of end users.
  • Offer best practices and recommendations.
  • Hold customer Immersion Experience sessions with first line workers.
  • Track progress and recommend next steps to sustain adoption

Drive Productivity with Wragby Acceler8

The introduction of new communication and collaboration technology such as Microsoft 365 in an organization drives transformation from traditional processes towards a new way of working. However, getting people to change long established norms and embrace the full functionalities of Microsoft 365 can be much harder than thought. To be successful, you need to properly manage the people-side impacts of such change. If you are ready to successfully drive adoption of your Microsoft 365 investment, Wragby acceler8 is here to help you navigate through the complexity of delivering Microsoft 365 and adopting the change it’s bringing across your entire organization.

Our service is designed for customers who are ready to embrace the full features of Microsoft 365, who have an existing Enterprise Agreement but would like to migrate to our CSP or who have an existing CSP. With the guidance of our customer success managers, our program helps you to:

  • Grow adoption and increase productivity for Microsoft 365 workloads such as Microsoft Teams, Yammer, One Drive, Outlook, SharePoint and more
  • Track adoption and growth across board
  • Get Guidance on licensing usage and adoption
  • Enable productivity through immersion sessions for end users with scenario-based learnings
